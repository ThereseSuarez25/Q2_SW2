Smartest Dog Breeds

Border Collie Border Collies are highly intelligent and highly trainable. It is a British breed and is considered to be the smartest dog breed. They live up to 17 years and can weigh from 12-20 kg.
Golden Retriever The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed. They are easy to train and can pick-up human emotions. They can live up to 12 years and can weigh from 25-34 kg.
Pembroke Welsh Corgi The Pembroke Welsh Corgi originated from Pembroke a city in Wales. They excell in agility, obedience, tracking, and herding. They are also known to be very loyal. They can live up to 12 years and can weigh from 10-14 kg.
Doberman Pinscher The Dobermann or Doberman Pinscher originated from Apolda, Germany. They are considered intelligent is their ability to gauge percieved threats in nearly any situation which is what makes them the premiere guard dogs. They can live up to 13 years and can weigh from 32-45 kg.
Beagle The Beagle originated from Great Britain and was developed primarily for hunting. They can live up to 15 years and can weigh from 9-11 kg.
Siberian Husky Siberian Huskies originated from Northeast Asia, it belongs to the Spitz genetic family. They are considrered to be of average intelligence. They can live up to 15 years and weigh from 16-27 kg.
Poodle Poodles originated from Germany. They're considered to be among the smartest dog breeds. They can live up to 15 years and can weigh from 20-32 kg.
Samoyed Samoyeds originated from Siberia, it takes its name from the Samoyedic people of Siberia. Their obedience and working intelligence is above average and have the ability to learn from their past mistakes. They can live up to 14 years and can weigh from 16-30 kg.
